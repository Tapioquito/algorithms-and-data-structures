

                        DATA STRUCTURES

        WHAT DO THEY DO?
    - Data structures are collection of values, the relationships among them,
    and the functions or operations that can be applied to the data;

        WHY SO MANY?
    - Different data structures excel at different things.
    - Some are highly specialized  while others (like arrays) are more generally used;
    
        WHY CARE?
    - The more time you spend as a developer, the more likely you'll need to use one of these data structures;
    - You've already worked with many of the unkowingly!
    - They are essential for INTERVIEWS!


    THERE IS NO 'BEST' DATA STRUCTURE!
    Every data structure has it's advantages in different sittuations
